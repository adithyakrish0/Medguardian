How to Add a Senior
  1. Make sure the senior citizen is already registered on the platform
  2. Enter their exact username
  3. Specify your relationship to them
  4. Add any relevant notes (optional)
  5. Click "Add Senior to Dashboard"
Requirements
  • The senior must have a registered account
  • Their account role must be "Senior Citizen"
  • They must not be already assigned to you
  • You must know their exact username
Need Help?

If you don't see the senior you're looking for, make sure they've registered first and that you have their correct username.
